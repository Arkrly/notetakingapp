Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is NoteStack free to use? +
Yes! NoteStack is completely free for college students. You get unlimited notes, cloud sync, and all features at no cost.
Can I access my notes on mobile? +
Absolutely! NoteStack is fully responsive and works on any device with a web browser. Just log in and your notes are there.
How secure are my notes? +
Very secure! We use JWT authentication and all data is stored in PostgreSQL with proper encryption. Only you can access your notes.
What if I forget my password? +
No worries! Use the password reset feature on the login page. We'll send you a secure link to create a new password.
Can I export my notes? +
Yes, you can export your notes in various formats. We're working on adding more export options in future updates.
